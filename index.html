Hi! I'm Amrit Bath,
an interdisciplinary researcher exploring fashion, AI, and education.

About Me

I'm a Physics undergraduate at the University of Manchester, working at the intersection of science, design, and technology. My interests lie in digital fashion, generative AI, and inclusive education. I've published on AI in fashion, co-developed curriculum reforms for accessibility, and led a national finalist pitch for L'Oréal Brandstorm. My work blends computational and creative thinking to reimagine how we build and learn.

Career Objectives & Research Interests

I'm motivated by meaningful innovation, particularly where ethics, design, and data converge. I aim to work in spaces where technology is used to empower, not exclude—with special focus on accessibility, sustainability, and AI literacy.
