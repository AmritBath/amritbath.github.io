Consultancy Report for Perricone MD in THG

My consultancy report for PerriconeMD in THG focused on industry strategy and digital techniques for Perricone MD's integration into their product line. I investigated the interaction between digital systems and customer behaviour and needs. I explored how innovative technologies such as AI and data analytics can enhance customer engagement and streamline operations. Adittionally, I interviewed industry experts to gather insights on best practices and emerging trends in digital consultancy.

Using both qualitative and quantitative analysis, I assessed the feasibility of implementing these digital strategies within PerriconeMD's existing framework. This project merged strategic consultancy with digital innovation, aligning technical solutions with business objectives.
