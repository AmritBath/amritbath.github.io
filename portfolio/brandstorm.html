L’Oréal Brandstorm: GroomGenius

For the 2025 L’Oréal Brandstorm competition, our team tackled the underdeveloped men’s skincare market by creating GroomGenius — an AI- and AR-powered mobile experience that empowers men to take control of their grooming with personalised, science-backed support.

We discovered that many men don't prioritise skincare due to a lack of education, confidence, and visibility in the beauty space. Our solution? A digital-first grooming companion that diagnoses skin and beard needs through a selfie scan, previews styling results in AR, and gamifies routines through micro-tutorials and rewards.

GroomGenius is designed to be inclusive, sustainable, and globally scalable—a platform where men are no longer an afterthought but a core beauty audience. It leverages L’Oréal’s existing AI technologies (like Skin Genius) and connects grooming to self-confidence and mental health through education and community.

Our cross-disciplinary team blended fashion, physics, and computer science to create a practical yet visionary solution. From research and stakeholder interviews to design, video production, and pitch delivery—we collaborated closely with industry mentors and user voices throughout.
