C++ Particle Detector Simulation

This project involved developing a particle detector simulation in C++, applying object-oriented design to model sub-detectors, particle types, and interactions. I implemented abstract base classes for particles (e.g. leptons, photons) and detectors (tracker, calorimeter, muon system).

The simulation processed events involving high-energy particles and logged their interactions through multiple detection layers. I focused on energy loss modelling, particle ID logic, and realistic detection thresholds. The final output included energy measurements, detection efficiency, and accuracy summaries.

I also submitted a technical report and packaged the codebase for download.
